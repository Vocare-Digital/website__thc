specialties

Outpatients

We can take ownership of the entire patient pathway from first attendance through to endoscopy, follow up and discharge as appropriate.

We will manage all administration that is associated with each patient episode, approving clinic letters, reviewing results of investigations and ensuring all outcomes are communicated to the patient and their general practitioner

Diagnostics

Diagnostic services (e.g. endoscopy) to relieve diagnostic waiting time pressures or following Totally Healthcare outpatient clinics to avoid any impact on local diagnostic services.

Day case and inpatient surgery

Totally Healthcare engaged surgeons, anaesthetists and surgical teams to carry out day cases and inpatient procedures within the hospital. Consultants provide an initial consultation with the patient and then return at an agreed date to carry out any surgical procedures, following local patient pathways through to discharge. A surgery-only service is also provided.

Waiting list review / validation

Assessment and prioritisation of patient waiting lists – to help the hospital manage its own lists.

  • Identifying high priority patients
  • Removing from lists patients who don’t need to be there
