Nurses and Allied Health Professionals

Who we are looking for

We are looking for nurses and allied health professionals to join our rapidly expanding network of highly motivated and dedicated healthcare professionals. By working with Totally Healthcare, you will enjoy the following benefits:

  • Confirmation of Professional Registration (i.e. NMC/NMBI)
  • Proof of right to work in the UK/Republic of Ireland
  • Photo identity documents (Passport or Driving License).
  • Two professional references, one of which must be from your current employer
  • Completion of an enhanced DBS application form
  • A completed Occupational Health form and evidence of health immunity status
  • Evidence of statutory and mandatory training
  • Evidence of professional indemnity i.e RCN.
  • The security of your personal data is paramount, which is why Totally Healthcare has processes in place to ensure that information is stored securely in accordance with GDPR Regulations.
