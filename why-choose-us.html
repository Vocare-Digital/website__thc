Why Choose Us?

About Totally Healthcare

Totally Healthcare is part of Totally plc – one of England’s leading healthcare providers, working across more than 230 locations nationwide to address the challenges of increased demand on health services by delivering high-quality care.

Totally Healthcare support Trusts/Hospitals to deliver patient waiting time initiatives across specialties throughout the UK and Ireland.

Our Management and clinical team have more than 40 years of healthcare experience within insourcing in the UK and Republic of Ireland and have helped treat more than 620,000 patients across all specialties.

How we do it

Identify Requirements

In order to provide a bespoke solution to your waiting list issues, we will meet with you to introduce the company and to discuss and identify your requirements.

Following these initial discussions, the team will put together a proposal for you.

Planning

The team at Totally Healthcare know that groundwork is the key to a successful initiative, so once an initial proposal has been agreed the team will perform a site visit<./p>

At this meeting, an assessment of the facilities will be undertaken as well as a walk-through of the patient journey to ensure that our service delivery mirrors the local service.

Mobilisation

Once the groundwork has been carried out, a mobilisation plan will be put into place including an agreed go-live date.

All contract arrangements are finalised prior to go-live, to ensure a smooth transition

Due to the extensive resource available, an initiative can be mobilised within a week.

Evaluation

Regular audit reports are produced for each hospital, sharing clinical outcomes and performance indication along with patient feedback.

Upon conclusion of each contract we embrace shard learning.
